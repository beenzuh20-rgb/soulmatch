SoulMatch Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SoulMatch. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how SoulMatch collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

By creating an account or using SoulMatch, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Full name or display name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth or age
  • Gender
  • Profile photos
  • Biography and profile details
  • Location (if you choose to provide it)

Account Information

  • Username
  • Password (stored securely in hashed form)
  • Account preferences

Messages

Messages exchanged between users may be stored to provide the service and investigate abuse.

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

SoulMatch uses your information to create and manage accounts, show profiles, match users, enable messaging, improve the service, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

SoulMatch does not sell your personal information. We may share information only with service providers, when required by law, or to protect safety and security.

5. Profile Visibility

Your profile information (name, photos, age, bio, etc.) is visible to other users. Please do not share sensitive personal information like your home address or financial details.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember preferences, and improve the platform. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us to make such requests.

9. Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account at any time. Some data may be retained for legal or safety reasons.

10. Children's Privacy

SoulMatch is for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of SoulMatch after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the website.

By using SoulMatch, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.
