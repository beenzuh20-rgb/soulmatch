SoulMatch Community Guidelines

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Welcome to SoulMatch. By using our platform, you agree to follow these Community Guidelines to keep the community safe and respectful.

1. Be Respectful

Treat every member with kindness. Do not harass, bully, use abusive language, or discriminate against others.

2. Be Honest

Your profile must accurately represent you. No fake profiles, stolen photos, or misrepresentation of age/identity.

3. Keep Conversations Appropriate

Respect boundaries. No unwanted sexual messages, harassment, or pressuring others.

4. No Scams or Fraud

Zero tolerance for asking for money, romance scams, or any fraudulent activity.

5. Protect Your Privacy

Never share your address, banking details, ID numbers, or passwords with other users.

6. Meet Safely

Always meet in public, tell a friend, and trust your instincts.

7. Appropriate Photos

Photos must not contain nudity, explicit content, or graphic violence.

8. Illegal Activities

Strictly prohibited: child exploitation, human trafficking, scams, blackmail, etc.

9. No Spam

No automated bots, mass messaging, or advertisements.

10. Respect Consent

"No" means "No". Respect when someone blocks you or asks you to stop.

11. Reporting Users

Use the Report button for fake profiles, harassment, scams, or inappropriate behavior.

12. Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban.

13. Our Commitment

We are committed to a safe and positive environment, but users are responsible for their own safety.

By using SoulMatch, you agree to these Community Guidelines.
